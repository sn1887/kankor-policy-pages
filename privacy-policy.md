# Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026

Kankor Prep WhatsApp Bot ("we", "our", "service") helps students prepare for Kankor by answering study questions over WhatsApp.

## 1. Information We Collect
- WhatsApp identifiers (such as your WhatsApp number and profile name, when provided by WhatsApp)
- Messages you send to the bot, including text and images
- Basic technical and operational logs used for reliability, security, and debugging

## 2. How We Use Information
- To receive messages and generate educational responses
- To operate, maintain, and improve the service
- To detect abuse, spam, and security issues

## 3. Legal Basis and Consent
By using this service, you consent to processing required to provide chatbot functionality.

## 4. Data Sharing

## 5. Data Retention

- Conversation records and logs may be deleted on a rolling schedule

## 6. Security
We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, but no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

## 7. Children and Students
This service is intended for educational support. If local law requires guardian consent for minors, users must obtain it before use.
